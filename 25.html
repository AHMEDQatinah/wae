Jujutsu Kaisen (ياب) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Jiji Akutami, and has been published in Weekly Shonen Jump since March 2018. The individual chapters of Jujutsu Kaisen have been collected and published by Shueisha, with fifteen volumes released as of March 2021. The story follows high school student Yuji Itadaori as he joins a secret organization of jujutsu magicians in order to kill a powerful curse called Ryomen Sukuna, of which Yuji becomes its host. The series is a sequel to a monthly four-season series called Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical School, which was published in Shueisha's Giga Jump from April to July 2017, and was later released under the title Jujutsu Kaisen 0 in December 2018.
