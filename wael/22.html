             It is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama.
              Its events take place in a fictional world where humans live within lands
               surrounded by three huge walls that protect them from giants that eat humans.

The manga series premiered in a magazine in Kodansha, Besatsu Shonen on September 9, 2009. 
It has been collected into 28 tankobun volumes as of April 2019. Attack on Titan has been a commercial success, 
and as of April 2019 nearly 90 million tankobun copies have been printed in worldwide (80 million in Japan and 10 million outside Japan),
 making it one of the best-selling manga series. She has won numerous awards, such as the Kodansha Manga Award, the Michelozzi Awards, 
 and the Harvey Award.
