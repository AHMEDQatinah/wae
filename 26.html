The Sniper (Japanese: ハンター×ハンター, Rumaji: Hantā Hantā, pronounced Hunter × Hunter) is a Japanese manga written by Yoshihiro Togashi, about a boy who aspires to be a hunter with the aim of searching for his disappeared father. After that, an anime series of the same title was formed, based on the first eighteen volumes of the manga. The manga has been a global success, with a unique story and innovative characters in the Togashi tradition. The manga has been featured in Shonen Jump since 1998, when it first appeared in the 14th issue, and has produced 24 volumes containing 390 chapters so far. The manga stopped publishing for several periods, due to Togashi's illness that prevented him from completing his work.
