Anime (Japanese: アニメ, romaji: anime) is a Japanese animation, 
    with worldwide fame. The Japanese word anime is an abbreviation of 
    the English word Animation, which refers to all animation, including cartoon.
     The first commercial anime dates back to 1917, and since then,
      anime production has continued, and its industry has flourished. Features of anime drawing,
       built in the sixties, on the works of the artist: Osamu Tezuka, and spread globally 
       in the late twentieth century, gaining it a local and international
        audience. Anime is spread and broadcast by television and cinema, direct to home 
        (now via DVD and Blu-ray discs), or via the Internet. It is divided into many and varied fields and classifications.
